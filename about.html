About HackTech

Engineering technology that solves real problems

HackTech was founded on one belief: technology should simplify businesses, not complicate them.

We engineer intelligent systems that combine software, artificial intelligence, IoT, automation and security into reliable solutions built for real-world environments.

Our story

From student innovation to an engineering company

HackTech started with a passion for solving practical problems through technology.

What began with software projects and research has grown into an engineering company building AI systems, IoT solutions, enterprise software and intelligent automation for businesses across Pakistan.

Today we continue to design technology that is practical, scalable and built to last — engineered, deployed and supported right here at home.
