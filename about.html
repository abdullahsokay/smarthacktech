Started with hardware. Grew into an ecosystem.

We were tired of watching Pakistani businesses depend on foreign black boxes that nobody here could open, repair or improve.

So we started soldering our own. Our flagship SOTMS grew out of that bench, locally-built tracking hardware and software behind our fleet-intelligence platform. But customers kept asking for more: a website, an app, a CCTV rollout, an AI chatbot, automation for the office. So we built the teams to deliver all of it.

Today HackTech is an IoT & AI ecosystem, connected devices, smart-home automation, AI & automation and security & surveillance, plus software development and professional training. Everything designed, built and supported right here in Pakistan.
