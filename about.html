About HackTech

We build Pakistan’s technology backbone — engineered at home

HackTech is an end-to-end technology company: software, AI, IoT, security, smart-home automation, training and an IoT components store. One Pakistani team across the whole stack — so your systems actually work together.

Our story

Started with hardware. Grew into an ecosystem.

We were tired of watching Pakistani businesses depend on foreign black boxes that nobody here could open, repair or improve.

So we started soldering our own. Our flagship grew out of that bench — SentinelEdge, a locally-made edge-AI device behind our fleet-intelligence platform. But customers kept asking for more: a website, an app, a CCTV rollout, an AI chatbot, automation for the office, parts for a project. So we built the teams to deliver all of it.

Today HackTech is a complete technology ecosystem — software development, AI & automation, IoT, security & surveillance, smart-home automation, professional training, and a nationwide IoT components store. Everything designed, built and supported right here in Pakistan.
